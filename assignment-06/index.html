Chicken Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 2 cups rotisserie chicken
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cans corn
  • 2 cans green beans
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 package of saltene crackers (crumbled)
  • optional: 3 cloves garlic

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425oF.
  2. Chop onion and celery and saute until translucent
  3. While onion and celery are cooking shred chicken
  4. Combine all ingredients in a 9x12 casserole dish
  5. Top with cheese and crumbled saltene crackers
  6. Place in oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown and bubbling
  7. Serve and enjoy while hot!
